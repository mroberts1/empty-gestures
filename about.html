Terminal Beach

Part diary, part logbook, part soapbox, this blog serves as a record of my everyday experience of screen-based networked communication technologies.

The title is inspired by J.G. Ballard’s classic short story collection The Terminal Beach (1964), although “terminal” here refers more to the computer terminal as the dominant screen interface of the digital age.
